FAQ

Common Parent Questions

We welcome children from 2.5 to 6 years old, with programs grouped by age so activities match each child's stage of development.

We're open Monday to Friday, 7:00 AM to 6:00 PM, with flexible drop-off and pick-up windows to fit busy family schedules.

Our centre uses secure access controls, constant staff supervision, and clear safety routines so every child stays protected throughout the day.

We serve fresh, balanced meals and snacks each day, and can accommodate common dietary needs and allergies with advance notice.

Educators share daily updates and regular progress notes, so you always have a clear picture of how your child is growing and learning.
